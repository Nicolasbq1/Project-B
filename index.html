Company Profile

We are developing a Bitcoin insurance policy for customers who invest in bitcoin exchanges which have been know to be vulnerable to hacking. According to Tyler Moore, an assistant cyber security professor at the University of Tulsaâ€™s Tandy School of Computer Science, 33% of stock exchanges have been infiltrated, and as Bitcoin gains awareness it becomes larger target for attack. Many trusted Bitcoin exchanges have been hacked such as Mt. Gox(450 million USD sustained losses) and more recently Bitfinex(72 million USD sustained losses) and were unable to properly reimburse customers. We sought a way to make these exchanges more secure for customers and created Bitsurance. A cross-analysis of past breaches in current bitcoin exchanges and categorization of security risk each exchange gives an informed way to detect potentially unstable exchanges. A mathematical Algorithm based on the probability of malicious hacking multiplied by the average losses expected calculates our rates. Premiums and monthly payment plans are then set for each of the major exchanges available for our customers. We look to bring trust and more confidence in investing in the evolutionary Bitcoin currency and wish to provide our customers with the utmost service.
